About, Carmel Convent School

Carmel Convent school established in 1971, is under the management of the institute of the Carmelite sisters of St. Teresa which is a Registered society. The School is a Minority Educational institution and hence enjoys all the privileges and prerogatives as enshrined under the Constitution of India.
